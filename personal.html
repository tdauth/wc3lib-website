Interests

Like many other people (hopefully) I am interested in various things. The following list gives you an overview about my primary interests:

  • Game modifications primarily for the games Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne, Gothic II, GTA San Andreas Multiplayer but also for old classics like Age of Empires II, Age of Mythology, Delta Force Black Hawk Down etc.
  • Programming: My favorite programming language is C++ or as some people call it C/C++ but I've also used Java, Go, Ruby, Scala and others as well as scripting languages for computer games such as JASS and vJass and more well known scripting languages as Bash.
